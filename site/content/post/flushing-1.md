Hydrant flushing is set to begin today for those on or near Lincoln Co. High School, Brock Drive, and Somerset Street.\
\
Flushing can cause discoloration or loss of water pressure for some residents. The discoloration is not a health hazard. Customers are cautioned to determine if the water is clear before washing clothes as staining may occur. If you experience discolored water run the cold water in all your faucets for approximately 5-10 minutes.\
