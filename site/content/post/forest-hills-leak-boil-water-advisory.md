There has been a water line break on Eastwood. 

Customers in the Forest Hills area may experience low to no water pressure due to the leak. There will be a boil water advisory for the area once the leak is fixed. There is no estimated time on how long the water will be off.



We apologize for the inconvenience and thank you for your patience.
