Per the executive order from the Governor of Kentucky, Stanford Waterworks may start disconnecting water service to customers with outstanding balances beginning November 6, 2020. Late fees will not be applied until January 1, 2021.



We are asking our customers to call the office about payment options for the past due balances for the period up to October 25, 2020.



To keep from having service disconnected, please make payment arrangements before the cut off date of November 6, 2020.
