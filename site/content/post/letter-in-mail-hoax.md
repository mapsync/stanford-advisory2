We have received numerous calls about customers receiving a letter in the mail that is not from us. \
\
If you receive a letter about 'line/pipe' insurance referencing buying insurance for your water lines, it is NOT from us. We are not sure if it is a hoax or a true company, but we are NOT affiliated with the company that is sending them.
