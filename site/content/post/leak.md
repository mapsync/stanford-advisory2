We have a water leak that we are working to get resolved. Many customers in the below areas may experience low to no water pressure.
Known areas affected include Rebecca Lane, HWY 1247, and part of US 27 past 1247 going toward Somerset. Trinity Lane looks to be affected as well. 
Customers that lose water will be under a boil water advisory once water is restored.
